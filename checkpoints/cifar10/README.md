# CIFAR-10 checkpoints

No trained checkpoints (`*.pth`) are shipped in this artifact.

Checkpoints are regenerated by running the reproducible CIFAR-10 pipeline:
- `scripts/run_cifar10_smoke.sh` produces a 1-epoch sanity checkpoint under
  `outputs/cifar10_smoke/.../models/`.
- `scripts/run_cifar10_full.sh` produces the full backdoored/clean checkpoints
  under `outputs/cifar10_full/.../models/`, which the downstream TOAP stages and
  the configs in `configs/cifar10/` consume.

This avoids shipping large binaries and avoids any risk of stale/identifying
metadata. No checkpoints were fabricated.
